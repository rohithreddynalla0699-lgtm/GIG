✅ You MUST only list:
  • •100% fresh, safe-to-eat food prepared or packaged on the same day
  • •Food that meets all FSSAI safety and hygiene standards
  • •Unsold surplus food — not expired, stale, or unfit for consumption

❌ You must NEVER list:

  • •Food stored in unhygienic conditions or improper temperatures
  • •Any food not fit for human consumption for any reason
  • •Food from previous days or reheated multiple times
